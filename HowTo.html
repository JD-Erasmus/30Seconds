How To Play Nam30

Game Setup

  • Gather 4 or more players, split into two teams.
  • Each round, one player describes 5 Namibian words to their team without using the word itself.
  • The team has 30 seconds to guess as many words as possible.

Game Rules

  1. No using translations or rhyming words.
  2. Gestures are allowed but no spelling out words!
  3. Each correct guess scores 1 point; team with most points wins.

Tips & Tricks

Think fast and creatively! Use cultural references to give hints, and keep an eye on the timer for that final word!
